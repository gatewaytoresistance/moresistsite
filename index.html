Welcome

Thanks for stopping by. This page is just a quick way to get the information you need to join the Ingress Resistance communities for both Illinois and Missouri.

Have an Android based smart phone? Like to compete? If so, Ingress is just for you. This game is described as the result of geeks combining smartphones and gpsr's for a fast based game that is a combination of "Risk" and "Capture the Flag". Sound interesting? Join one of the Resistance communities and we will help you get going.

Download the app from the PlayStore, request an invite, and join the Resistance faction (blue). You'll probably need a G+ account, but heck, those are free too. It's that easy.

Which community should I join? Many of the Metro East players join both the Illinois and Missouri Resistance communities, Southern Illinois Players usually join the Southern Illinois and Illinois communities. Your choice. Heck, join all of them. You are welcome in all the communities. We'll offer you advice and guidance and if necessary will meet up with you to help you progress through the Ingress levels. Don't be afraid to ask for help. We've all had to at one time or another. Come on, what do you have to lose?

Show Me Resistance (MoRe)

To apply for Show Me Resistance community membership:

  1. Go to the Gateway to Resistance G+ community and click the red Join community button.
  2. Create a post in the community that includes a screenshot of your Ingress scanner with the faction comms open and your player name visible. If you have difficulty with this, create a post asking for help.
  3. Wait for a moderator to comment on your Google+ post with further instructions.
  4. Expect to be added to circles by one or more moderators and asked a few questions about yourself.
  5. Add the moderator(s) to one of your circles. This is necessary to ensure that you see communications from the moderator.
  6. Once you have completed the approval process with a moderator, you will receive an invitation to the Show Me Resistance New Player community.
  7. Please make a introduction post in the roll call section of the Show Me Resistance New Player community with your username, area of play, and current level.
